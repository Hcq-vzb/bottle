PET Stretch Blow Molding Energy Efficiency 2026

Published 2026-06-14 · KlWL Machinery

Understanding PET stretch blow molding machine requirements helps plant managers and buyers make confident equipment decisions. This guide summarizes practical criteria aligned with current industry practice.

Energy Costs Drive Machine Selection

Electricity for preform heating and high-pressure air remains a major OPEX item for PET bottle producers. Modern PET stretch blow molding machines use servo-based stretching and optimized lamp zones to cut kWh per thousand bottles.

Servo vs. Pneumatic Stretch Systems

Servo stretch rods offer programmable motion profiles, reducing wall thickness variation and scrap. Plants report 10–18% energy savings when upgrading from older pneumatic lines.

Preform Heating Optimization

Zone-controlled infrared ovens with PID feedback heat only what each mold cycle needs. Uniform preform temperature improves clarity for water and CSD bottles while shortening cycle time.

Maintenance and Air Recovery

Leak-free high-pressure circuits and heat recovery on compressors further lower consumption. Schedule mold vent cleaning and check valve seals quarterly.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much energy can a retrofit save?

Typical upgrades show 12–20% reduction depending on bottle size and output.

Does energy efficiency affect bottle quality?

Better heating control often improves consistency and reduces thin-spot rejects.

What bottle types benefit most?

High-volume water and beverage lines see the fastest payback on servo upgrades.
