What is the development trend of fully automatic high-end blow molding machines?

Release time:

2024-04-09

​​​​​​​With the development of the domestic plastic bottle market, plastic bottle blowing machines have also developed rapidly. They are widely used in beverage, medicine, cosmetics and food industries, and have attracted more manufacturers to choose bottle blowing machines to replace previous bottle making tools.

With the development of the domestic plastic bottle market, plastic bottle blowing machines have also developed rapidly. They are widely used in beverage, medicine, cosmetics and food industries, and have attracted more manufacturers to choose bottle blowing machines to replace previous bottle making tools.

Among many products, multi-layer co-extrusion hollow bottle blowing machine is a hollow bottle blowing machine with great development prospects. As the proportion of multi-layer blow-molded high-barrier hollow products in the field of hollow products increases, multi-layer blow-molded products are developing rapidly not only in the food packaging industry, but also in chemicals, cosmetics, medicine and health and other industrial packaging. Therefore, the development of bottle blowing machine is due to its excellent characteristics, and it will definitely reach its peak in the next few years.

However, with the wide application, the requirements are also increasing. At present, the pharmaceutical industry has two urgent needs for bottle blowing equipment. First, due to the continuous rise in the price of plastic raw materials, companies are always thinking about how to reduce packaging costs, so they need to use bottle blowing equipment to produce lighter bottles; second, due to the rapid changes in the market, manufacturers have to respond flexibly to the increasingly shorter life cycle of medicines. These two urgent needs have posed a big problem for bottle blowing equipment suppliers.

Therefore, in the subsequent development process, if bottle blowing machine companies want to achieve long-term development, they must face these problems. The industry must give full play to its own advantages to seize the market, and then continuously strengthen technological innovation and establish a good brand image to stabilize the market.
